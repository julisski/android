TODO 6 · Row + weight(1f) MainActivity.kt → TaskRow

One Modifier.weight(1f) is what pushes the controls to the edge

A TaskRow is Checkbox · [title+notes] · priority · Delete in a Row. Flip the switch to give the middle Column Modifier.weight(1f) — it eats the leftover width and shoves the priority label and Delete button to the far right. Without it, everything clumps on the left.

Middle column has Modifier.weight(1f)

the row, as Kotlin

⚠️ Common mix-up: weight only exists inside a Row/Column scope, and it divides the leftover space after fixed-size children (the checkbox, the buttons) are measured. One child with weight(1f) = "take everything that's left."
